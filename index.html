Verify your dollars in seconds and protect yourself from fraud

Dollar Verify is a mobile app that helps you validate cash dollars by comparing the data on your bills with the real ones and giving you tips on how to recognize them. It is a fast, accurate and reliable app that protects you from fraud.

Select the denomination of the bill

Look at the bill and choose the denomination in the list of bills in the app, either 100, 50, 20, 10, 5, 2 or 1 dollars. Then go to the next step.

Select the year of the series of the bill

Look for the year of the series on the bill, which is located in the lower left corner of the front. The app shows you an illustrative image to help you locate it. Then enter the year in the list that appears in the app.

DATA 100%

Enter the code of the bill

This process consists of three steps: select the first two letters of the code, enter the eight-digit numeric code and enter the last character of the code. These data are located in the upper right corner of the front of the bill. The app provides you with a form with three fields for you to enter them individually.

95%

Check the result and tips

If the bill is good, the app will show you a screen telling you that the bill is authentic and will offer you a series of tips and features of the bill that you should check to confirm it. If the bill is fake, the app will alert you and give you some recommendations to avoid being a victim of fraud.
